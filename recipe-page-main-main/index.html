Simple Omelette Recipe


An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 Minutes
  • Cooking: 5 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meets, herbs


Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a browl, beat the ehhs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture
  2. Heat the pan: Place a nonstick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Till the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fellings(optional): When the ehhs begin to set at the edges but are still sightly runny in the moddle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As tje omelette continus to cook, cargully ft one edge and fold it over the fillings, let it cool for another minute, then side it anto a plate
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional sald and pepper if needed
